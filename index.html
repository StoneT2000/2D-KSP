This is a 2D recreation of KSP made with HTML, JS, p5.JS, CSS. (Fairly reduced version)
Press W,A,S,D to move camera around
Press - or + to zoom in our out
Press the arrow keys to add velocity in that direction to the spacecraft (The skyblue square)
Press , or . to slow or speed up the game time
Click on any object to automatically follow it
